    Ours will truly be your tribe

    As an on- and offline community, Mateship is revolutionizing the way in which veterans can connect, inform and support each other.

    “When I left the Army I suffered a loss of identity and community that left me feeling isolated and unable to connect with others. I found myself between the military and civilian worlds, but part of neither.
    Meeting and talking to other veterans who feel the same way has helped me immensely, but finding them has been hard.
    To goal of mateship is to make it easy to find and connect with other veterans, to share your stories and develop a trusted network of peers who are just like you”
    Chris Moran Community Manager at Mateship

    Our mission

    Each year more than 5000 people discharge from the ADF.

    Our mission is to work with the veteran community to ensure that: no veteran feels left alone, information is easily shared and newly separated individuals and families have access to the insight of those who have been there before.

    Our why

    We are a social enterprise, which means our community (that’s you) is behind everything that we do.

    What gets us out of bed in the morning is the dream where every person who leaves the ADF feels connected to a larger community and can reach out whenever they need to.

    Our recipe for change

    People power:

    we work in direct partnership with our community, from everything to product design to funding

    Giving culture:

    our community voluntarily contribute their time and expertise to support other veterans faced with similar experiences

    Transparency:

    we enable parents to make informed decisions by providing unbiased access to community-authored resources

    Innovation:

    we use cutting edge technology to ensure our community has the support the need, when they need it.
